Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Interval Buddy does not collect, store, or process personal data directly from users.

Third-Party Services

Interval Buddy uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob, for displaying ads
  • Google Play Billing, for in-app purchases

These services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. You can review Google’s Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Collection

Interval Buddy itself does not collect or store personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location data, or account information.

Data Retention

Since Interval Buddy does not collect or store personal data directly, no personal user data is retained by the app.

Any data that may be collected by third-party services, such as Google AdMob or Google Play Billing, is handled and retained according to those services’ own privacy policies.

Data Deletion

Since Interval Buddy does not collect or store personal data directly, there is no personal user data stored by the app to delete.

Users may uninstall the app at any time to stop using the app. To manage or delete data associated with Google services, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

User Rights

Users may stop using Interval Buddy at any time by uninstalling the app from their device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
